\begin{frame}{Incredible certitude}

In economics, however, in practice, use the point estimates as a plug-in for the true parameter and the model is analyzed as-if the true parameters are known \citep{Manski.2019}.

  	\heading{Consequences}\vspace{0.3cm}
  	\begin{itemize}\setlength\itemsep{1em}
  	\item fragile findings are accepted as facts
  	\item dueling certitudes stifle constructive debate
  	\item knowledge gaps are not identified
    \item trade-off between model complexity and uncertainties is ignored
    \item  impossible to identify models used out of their area of reliability
  	\end{itemize}

\end{frame}
